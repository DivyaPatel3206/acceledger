The Problem

CAs Buried Under Manual Voucher Verification

Financial integrity officers are stretched thin. The human gap in 100% transaction coverage is not a resource issue — it's a structural one.

⚠️
The Professional Challenge

CAs are tasked with ensuring financial integrity, yet are often buried under a mountain of manual voucher verification — with no time to inspect every entry.

🔍
The Human Gap

Statistical sampling exists because humans cannot physically review 100% of transactions without fatigue-led error. This leaves a systematic blind spot.

📊
The Scale Issue

CA firms manage 10–200 client companies. A single CA reviewing hundreds of vouchers daily will inevitably miss patterns that span clients or repeat across time.

💸
The Risk

Fraud and clerical errors hide in the "unseen" 90% of data — only surfacing after ITC disallowance or a tax audit.

System Architecture

How the Platform Connects

AccuLedger connects company setup, ledger masters, voucher entry, HSN/GST mapping, and AI compliance modules through a shared SQLite database. Every saved voucher is automatically run through the fraud detection pipeline.

📥
Input Layer

Manual voucher entry via web interface. HSN code auto-suggest with GST rate lookup.

⚙️
Processing Layer

Tax logic maps HSN codes to GST rates. Vouchers stored in accounting DB. GSTIN verified.

🤖
AI Scrutiny Engine

XGBoost × 0.5 + IsolationForest × 0.5 ensemble · SHAP feature attribution · Duplicate check · Compliance rules

📊
Output Layer

Dashboards, risk flags, "Why Flagged?" backtracking, GSTR-2B reconciliation, Merkle audit chain.

AI Scrutiny Engine — 5-Layer Pipeline
L1
Rules Check

GSTIN format · missing fields · HSN-GST mismatch · cash limits

L2
XGBoost Classifier

12-feature vector · SHAP attribution · fraud probability 0–100

L3
Isolation Forest

Unsupervised anomaly detection · per-company model · n_estimators=200

L4
Duplicate Check

Invoice + party + amount fuzzy matching · flags reformatted duplicates

L5
Ensemble Score

XGBoost × 0.5 + IsoForest × 0.5 → combined 0–100 risk score

The Core Innovation

AccuLedger's USP is embedding intelligence directly into daily accounting operations — making audits faster, decisions smarter, and business finance management more reliable. XGBoost × 0.5 + IsolationForest × 0.5 ensemble gives the best of supervised learning (known fraud patterns) AND unsupervised anomaly detection (novel patterns). Unlike standalone tools, AccuLedger sits inside the accounting process and makes every entry smarter, automatically.
